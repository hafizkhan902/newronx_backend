üì± APPLICATION OVERVIEW
=======================
StudentMate is a comprehensive student collaboration platform that enables users to:
- Share and collaborate on innovative ideas
- Connect with other students and professionals
- Manage projects with NDA protection
- Communicate through real-time messaging
- Receive notifications and updates
- Customize their experience with themes and privacy settings

üèóÔ∏è ARCHITECTURE & TECHNOLOGY STACK
==================================
- Backend: Node.js with Express.js framework
- Database: MongoDB with Mongoose ODM
- Authentication: JWT tokens + Passport.js + Google OAuth 2.0
- Real-time: Socket.IO for live communication
- File Storage: Cloudinary for image/file uploads
- Email: Nodemailer for email services
- Caching: Redis for session management and caching
- Security: Helmet.js, rate limiting, input validation
- Architecture: MVC (Model-View-Controller) pattern
